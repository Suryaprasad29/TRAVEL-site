Popular Places

Eiffel Tower, Paris
  •  400 reviews

The Eiffel Tower has become a symbol of Paris and France and is one of the most recognizable landmarks in the world. It is a must-see for visitors to Paris, and is considered as one of the most romantic place in the world.

Machu Picchu, Peru
  •  400 reviews

Machu Picchu is considered a sacred place by the local people and is also considered as one of the new seven wonders of the world, it offers an incredible mix of architectural, natural and cultural beauty, as well as a glimpse into the mysterious and sophisticated Inca civilization.

Acropolis, Athens
  •  400 reviews

The Acropolis is situated on a high hill in the center of Athens and offers stunning views of the city and the surrounding area. The citadel is made up of several ancient buildings, monuments, and ruins, all of which are well-preserved and offer a glimpse into the ancient past.

Bali, Indonesia
  •  400 reviews

Bali is also home to many ancient temples and religious sites, including the iconic Uluwatu Temple, which sits on a cliff above the ocean and offers stunning views. The island also has many traditional villages, such as Ubud, which are known for their arts, crafts, and dances.

Dubai, United Arab Emirates
  •  400 reviews

Dubai is a city located in the United Arab Emirates, known for its modern architecture, luxury shopping, and vibrant nightlife. It is the most populous city in the UAE and a major global business and tourism destination.

Bhutan
  •  400 reviews

Bhutan is a small, landlocked country located in the Eastern Himalayas, known for its stunning natural beauty, rich culture, and commitment to preserving its traditional way of life. It is often referred to as the "Land of the Thunder Dragon" and is known for its policy of Gross National Happiness, which aims to balance economic development with the preservation of culture and the environment.

Havana, Cuba
  •  400 reviews

Havana is the capital and largest city of Cuba, known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and colonial architecture. The city is located on the northwest coast of the island, and it's considered as one of the most significant and recognizable cities in the Caribbean.

Moskva, Russia
  •  400 reviews

Moscow is the capital and largest city of Russia, known for its rich history, stunning architecture, and cultural heritage. It is the political, economic, and cultural center of the country and one of the most populous cities in Europe.
